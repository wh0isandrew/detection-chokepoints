## Key Distinction: Source vs Generated

**Edit these:** `chokepoints/`, `sigma-rules/`, `iok-rules/`, `emulation/`, `_layouts/`, `_includes/`, `assets/`

**Never edit directly:** `_chokepoints/`, `_data/chokepoints.yml`, `assets/js/search-index.json` — all regenerated by `aggregate.py`
